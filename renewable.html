Renwable Energy Solutions

Overveiw

Make the switch to clean, sustainable power with our renewable energy services. From solar panel installation to EV charger setups and battery storage systems, we help homeowners and businesses reduce energy costs and environmental impact.

What to Expect

Installations typically take 1–2 days depending on system size. You’ll start seeing savings on energy bills right away, with options to monitor usage in real-time through mobile apps.

What's included:

  • Solar PV system design and installation
  • Battery storage integration for energy independence
  • EV charger supply and fitting (home or workplace)
  • Grid connection and DNO notification
  • Ongoing support and maintenance options

Proccess:

  • Consultation: We assess your energy needs, property layout, and goals.
  • Design & Quotation: A custom solution is created with a transparent cost breakdown.
  • Installation: All work is completed by MCS-certified electricians.
  • Commissioning & Handover: We test the system, help you understand usage, and file any required registrations.
