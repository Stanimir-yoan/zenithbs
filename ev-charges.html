EV Charges

Start Your Electric Vehicle Journey Today

With environmental concerns and rising fuel costs pushing millions toward electric vehicles (EVs), a reliable home charging solution is essential. A professionally installed EV charger offers convenience, efficiency, and safety, allowing you to power up your vehicle at home with ease. Our certified electricians provide customized installations that meet UK safety standards, ensuring your charger is perfectly suited for your home or business needs.

Eco-Friendly Choice

Charging your EV at home reduces your carbon footprint and supports a sustainable future.

Cost Savings

Home charging is often cheaper than public stations, and government grants can offset installation costs.

Reliable Performance

Our installations meet IET Wiring Regulations, ensuring safe and efficient charging for years to come.

What’s Involved in EV Charger Installation?

Installing an EV charger is a straightforward process when handled by professionals. We ensure your charging station is set up safely and tailored to your property’s electrical capacity and your vehicle’s requirements.

Site Survey

We assess your property’s electrical system, parking setup, and charging needs to recommend the ideal charger type and location.

Expert Installation

Our team installs your charger with precision, including wiring, safety devices like RCDs, and secure mounting for optimal performance.

Once installed, we perform rigorous testing to confirm your charger operates safely and efficiently. You’ll receive a detailed compliance report, user instructions, and advice on maintenance to keep your system running smoothly. We also guide you through available grants, like the Electric Vehicle Homecharge Scheme, to reduce costs.
