House Rewiring

Everything You Need to Know About Electrical Rewires

Rewiring is one of the most difficult home maintenance projects, requiring work in every room of your house. The electrical system, powered by wires typically buried in the walls, must be replaced every 25-30 years to ensure safety. All socket outlets, light switches, and electrical items like your stove and boiler depend on these cables. Substandard or improperly installed electrics can cause fires and electric shocks.

At Zenith BS Electrical, we rewire your home to avoid potential dangers and guarantee everything is safe and current. We replace old or unsafe cables, which can deteriorate over time, to prevent malfunctions. Our team prepares your property for the work to begin, ensuring a smooth process.
