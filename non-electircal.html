Non-Electrical Work

Overveiw

We go beyond just electrics to offer small-scale non-electrical tasks that support your projects. Whether you need help mounting a TV, installing a shelf, or coordinating with other trades during a renovation, we’re here to make it easy.

What to Expect

We keep things simple, clear, and tidy. Our non-electric work is ideal for small tasks that don’t require hiring a full separate team.

What's included:

  • Minor repair and installation work
  • Basic carpentry or wall-mounting tasks
  • Site preparation or patching after electrical work
  • Coordination with plumbers, builders, and decorators

Proccess:

  • Discuss the Job: Let us know what you need — if we can do it, we’ll say so clearly.
  • Work Execution: We’ll schedule the job and complete it with the same attention to detail as our electrical services.
  • Follow-Up: Need anything else? We’re happy to offer continued support or bring in trusted partners.
