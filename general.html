General Electrical Services

Overveiw

From simple fixes to full-scale installations, we handle all general electrical work with professionalism and precision. Whether you're upgrading lighting, adding extra sockets, or planning a new kitchen, we’re here to help.

What to Expect

We aim to cause as little disruption as possible and are happy to work around your schedule. You’ll always get clear communication and reliable service from start to finish.

What's included:

  • Lighting and socket installation
  • Appliance wiring
  • Outdoor electrics and garden lighting
  • Fault finding and repairs
  • Full or partial electrical installations

Proccess:

  • Free Consultation or Quote: We discuss your project in detail to understand your goals.
  • Scheduling & Preparation: We arrange a suitable time and make sure materials are sourced.

  • Testing & Sign-off: Everything is tested to ensure safety and performance.
