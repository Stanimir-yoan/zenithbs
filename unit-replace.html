Unit Replacement

Overveiw

Upgrading your consumer unit (also known as a fuse box) is one of the most effective ways to improve your home or business's electrical safety. We install modern units equipped with circuit breakers and RCD protection for total peace of mind.

What to Expect

Power may need to be switched off during installation, typically for a few hours. We’ll coordinate with you to choose the most convenient time, and we’ll leave the space tidy once we’re done.

What's included:

  • Inspection of current unit and circuits
  • Removal and safe disposal of old unit
  • Installation of new 17th/18th Edition compliant consumer unit
  • Full system testing and NICEIC certification

Proccess:

  • Initial Check: We evaluate your current consumer unit and determine if an upgrade is necessary.
  • Quotation & Scheduling: You’ll receive a quote with a timeframe — usually, replacements take a day or less.
  • Installation: Old equipment is removed, and the new unit is installed safely and cleanly.
  • Testing & Certification: All circuits are tested and certified for safety and compliance.
